Project Making Process

Step 1: [Arranging the wodden stick and screws ]

Arrange/Cut the wodden stick with same size and take the 11 screws and put it into the wodden stick with the help of drill.And put 5 screws in one side which will be getting positive current and 6 screws for another stick which will getting negative current.

Step 2: [Solding of Wire]

After putting screws in the stick join the screws with positive and negative screws respectively.

Step 3: [Testing]

Prepare the suitable appartous and be prepared with the safety(wearing the rubber gloves).Then,put the prepared two wodden stick into the electrolyte water i.e-NACL,NAOH or KOH.Now connect the wire to the dc current or to the A/C to D/C convertor.And after some bubbles will come which is h2 gas.
